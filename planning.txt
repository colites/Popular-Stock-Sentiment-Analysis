The AI will go through reddit subforums such as wallstreetbets, to figure out which stocks are being hyped up.
It will classify them as a pump and dump or as a good opportunity for hype investing.


## Accurate Ticker Filtering:

Currently have these steps in place for filtering:

- Filter by Uppercase
- Filter by $ sign on words
- Filter by length of Words having to be 1 < words < 5
- Filter by a blacklist of common words that fit above but are not tickers
